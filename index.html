Before You Come In

Studio
Policies

A few things to know before your appointment — so we can give you the best experience possible.

01
Deposit Required

A $25 non-refundable deposit is required to confirm your booking. This goes toward your total balance on the day of your appointment.

02
Cancellations & Reschedules

Cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance to avoid losing your deposit. Use the link in your confirmation email to make changes.

03
Grace Period

There is a 10-minute grace period for late arrivals. Appointments may be cancelled after this point and the deposit will be forfeited.

04
Come Prepared

Please arrive with your inspiration photos ready and your wig clean, pre-plucked, and pre-bleached. This helps us make the most of your appointment time.

05
No Extra Guests

To maintain a calm studio environment, please do not bring additional guests unless previously approved. Children must be accompanied by another adult.

06
Questions?

If you're unsure about anything before your visit, reach out via the contact form below. We're happy to help you prepare for a smooth appointment.
